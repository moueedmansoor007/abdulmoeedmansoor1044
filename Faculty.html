Dean Message


Dr. Tahir Hussian

It gives me immense pleasure to invite you to the Website of Software Engineering Department, MUET. Software Engineering Department used to conduct its programs in conjunction with the Department of Computer Systems Engineering untill 2009, after which the Department of Software Engineering got its own building. The department offers Bachelors of Engineering (B.E), Masters of Engineering (M.E) and Doctorate (Ph.D.) degrees in Software Engineering. Our graduates are already serving in the reputable public and private sector Software organizations both locally and internationally. The department provides a congenial environment for carrying out edge-cutting research and experimental work with modern state-of-the-art laboratories in collaboration with our top-notch faculty members. Our faculty is highly qualified and always ready to guide the students in any academic related problem, where as the courteous administrative staff is always attentive to address the students' problems. Please explore the department of Software Engineering, I am sure you will love to be a part of this department.
Dr. NTahir Hussian
Dean of Faculty Computing
